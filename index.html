My food website!

This website will tell you all about my favorite foods & show you some pictures!

Tacos

Fish tacos make for a very fresh and quick dish. Fun to eat and fun to make, they are a more authentic Mexican staple that one could enjoy and are versatile enough that eating them never gets boring.

Tofu in tomato sauce

Tofu in tomato sauce is one of my favorite Vietnamese foods. It has a nice mix of spice and sweetness, and texturally is balanced between crispy tofu and a smooth sauce.

Scrambled eggs

Scrambled eggs are what I eat to start my day. They, like tofu in tomato sauce, have a nice balance between textures and flavors, and are quite versatile.
